Thakadu Self Catering Holiday Accommodation

Bush Lodge (Self Catering) (GAME NEARBY) In Mookgophong (Naboomspruit), Waterberg, Limpopo

Thakadu Game Lodge invites you to come and taste the Bushveld in an unforgettable experience of its natural wonders. Thakadu Game Lodge was established in 1993 by developing indigenous Bushveld in an enclosed nature conservation area.

 
Retreat to splendid seclusion on the foot of the Waterberg. Come for a weekend or a week and experience the African Bush at its finest.

• Self Catering Chalets • Guided Game Drives • Hiking and Bird viewing • Swimming Pool • Conferences • Christmas Dinners and Lunches • Family Reunions • Weddings
