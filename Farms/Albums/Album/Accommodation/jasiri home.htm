Accommodation

ACCOMMODATION

Come join us at our lodge or in a comfy chalet

We offer premium accommodation for all your hunting excursions. Stay in our hunting lodge which features a bar, swimming pool to cool down in and a boma to put your feet up, make a huge bon-fire, and trade a few stories. Alternatively you can stay in one of our chalets tucked away in the bush for a night of quiet relaxation.

Our hunting camp can accommodate 12 guests, special arrangements can be made for kids sleeping on mattresses/ or sharing. The hunting camp is self-catering but includes a fully equipped kitchen, electricity, 3 bathrooms, a swimming pool, mini-bar, dartboard, pool table, 2 fridges and a freezer, 2 dining room areas and an outside boma with seating options. We advise guests to bring along their own towels. We sell small and large bags of ice. We stock the boma with wood for your convenient use, but you can buy extra wood from us. The hunting house has 3 x 2 sleeper rooms, one bathroom, sitting room and a kitchen. It also includes a boma and a swimming pool. The lodge hosts 9 chalets, accommodating 18 guests, but again special arrangements can be made with children. The lodge chalets are not self-catering; however a coffee bar is available. Guests can fulfill their needs at the restaurant. We allow large groups that book the hunting camp to have guests over in the lodge chalets, where then all is self-catering.
